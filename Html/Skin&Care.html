Dermatology Department of Prakash Hospital

Earlier known as the practice of skin healing, Dermatology has become the most competitive specialty in medicine with the broader incorporation of Cosmetology in the present times. From perfect glowing skin to treating severe acne scars, the power of cosmetic treatment is invincible. Our dermatology department is exclusively established to provide comprehensive diagnostics and varied treatment options giving a healthy skin. We strive to introduce advanced and revolutionary skincare services suiting different skin types and pre-existing conditions like acne, allergies, rosacea, eczema, lupus, and psoriasis. Our experts render the best diagnoses and management for the conditions affecting, skin, nails, and hair.

Why Choose Us?

Prakash Hospital, Noida in association with Claire Derma has brought a range of treatments from cosmetic dermatology, hair problems, nail diseases, acne scar surgery, botox, fillers, & facial augmentation, facial rejuvenation, platelet-rich plasma therapy, chemical peels, pediatric dermatology, removal of wart, moles, skin tags, lipoma excision, sebaceous cyst excision and much more. The department is headed by Dr. Mohna Chauhan, one of the youngest and most experienced dermatologists in Noida.

She specializes in Venereology & Leprosy dermatology, an expert dermatosurgeron, and aesthetic dermatologist offering quality and compassionate care to the patients. At Prakash Hospital, we deliver dermatology consultations for the inpatients offering expert counseling sessions and superior quality care for scar-less recovery.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    If you have developed any abnormal symptoms affecting your skin, hair, or nails, consult a dermatologist immediately. These days, you can consult a cosmetologist or a dermatologist for enhancing the quality of your skin and get treated for pre-existing conditions as well.

    Some of the common treatments including acne, fungal infections, hair loss, nail diseases, and skin allergies are well-treated by dermatologists. Also, you can visit the specialist for minimally invasive procedures like biopsies, chemical peels, cosmetic injections, hair removal and restoration, or laser procedures.
