Orthopedics & Traumatology Department of Prakash Hospital

Orthopedics is a medical specialty that deals with diagnosing, preventing and treating conditions related to bones and muscles. The Orthopedics & Traumatology Department of Prakash Hospital, Noida is fully facilitated in managing diseases, injuries, and conditions of the musculoskeletal system including the joints, ligaments, tendons and nerves for both children and adults. The department is one of the advanced units in the region and has the best bone specialists, renowned surgeons for replacement surgeries and advanced diagnostics facilities with HEPA filters, and DEXA scans for accurate measurement of bone density and strength.

Why Choose Us?

The Orthopedics & Traumatology Department is dedicated to delivering personalized and comprehensive orthopedics care to patients using advanced healthcare technologies and techniques supported by a team of experienced and highly trained professionals. Our team believes in a collaborative approach and efforts for treating patients, the expertise of orthopedic surgeons, physicians, radiologists, and physiotherapist contributes to healing and rehabilitation at its best level.

Every need of our patients is addressed by the experts providing cutting-edge services and minimally-invasive solutions for almost all types of orthopedic conditions. Other providers who specialize in the care of orthopedic conditions also help specialists to provide optimal care. Our Department is well-established with the latest technology equipment for improving the clinical and diagnostic skills of the experts delivering quality care in Joint replacement, Arthroscopies & Spine.

    • Services we offer:

    Nonsurgical treatment:

    Several musculoskeletal conditions can be well-treated without the need for surgery. Our orthopedics evaluate the issues and prescribe a tailored treatment involving medication, exercise, rehabilitation or alternative therapies in favor of the patient’s health conditions. In cases, where non-surgical treatments don’t bring out the best results, going for surgical options is considered a good option.

    We provide non-surgical treatment for common to severe orthopedic conditions like:

    Back Pain:

    One of the initial treatments for chronic back pain is exercise, which is planned under the guidance of a physician and spine physical therapist. A back brace is provided to patients who find it comfortable and effectively reduces their pain. Chronic back pain can be alleviated by various nonsurgical treatments, including acupuncture, massage, biofeedback therapy, laser therapy, and electrical nerve stimulation.

    Slip Disc:

    One should certainly avoid all types of strenuous sports and work activities. It is believed that inflammation in the nerves and soft tissue surrounding a herniated disc is responsible for some of the associated pain. However, nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory medications can be used to treat the inflammation effectively, which can help reduce swelling and alleviate the resulting pain.

    Other conditions that can be managed through non-surgical means:

  • Osteoporosis
  • Arthritis
  • Fractures
  • Torn Ligaments
  • Sprains and strains
  • Sports Injuries

    • Surgical treatment:

    Being one of the advanced orthopedic & traumatology Departments in Noida, we have an operational team to perform various types of surgeries, that include:

    Arthroscopy:

    The procedure is performed using special cameras and equipment to evaluate, detect and treat conditions inside a deformed/ inflamed joint.

    Joint replacement:

    The surgery can be a partial, total or revision procedure where an arthritic or damaged joint is removed and replaced with an artificial joint called a prosthesis.

    If you experience mild to excruciating pain in your bones, muscles or joints, it is recommended to visit an orthopedic.
