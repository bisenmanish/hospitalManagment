Physiotherapy Department of Prakash Hospital

Physiotherapy is mainly concerned with the assessment, maintenance, and restoration of physical function and disability. “Physical Therapy” itself is a broader field consisting of professionals including physiotherapists, occupational therapists, speech therapists, orthopedic therapists, rehabilitation psychologists, and nutritionists work as a team to enhance physical and mental functioning for the improved quality of life of patients, especially the ones affected by the chronic or severe conditions.

At Prakash Hospital, Noida the Department of Physiotherapy works in a collaborative manner with other medical specialties to deliver patient-centric rehabilitation care and services to the needful. Our team believes not in treating but in healing people to the core! The art of Physiotherapy is all about healing the patient offering strength and confidence using personalized touch and our team excels in building a patient connection that sets us apart from the others!

Why Choose Us?

In Noida, our Department of Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation is known to be one of the best in the region for providing tailored plans to the patients. Our physical rehabilitation plans are applicable in all areas of modern medicine offering the best physiotherapeutic treatment to the patients both pre-operation and post-operation. The department works under the supervision and guidance of Dr. V.S Chauhan, an eminent Orthopedic Surgeon renowned for his expertise in the field. Has contributed to expand the department and gave it an edge by starting an institute dedicated to physiotherapy in Greater Noida, “ Prakash Institute of Physiotherapy, Rehabilitation and Allied Medical Science (PIPRAMS)”

At Prakash Hospital, Noida you are going to get treated under a team of competent and qualified physiotherapists who specializes in orthopedics, sports medicine, critical care, cardiac sciences, oncology, pediatric, medicine, gynecology, and others.

Conditions we treat:

Our team provides holistic physical rehabilitation through our program, customized for all individuals as per their requirements. People suffering from can visit the center for:

  • Back Pain
  • Shoulder Pain
  • Postoperative Stiffness
  • Paralysis (Hemiplegia, paraplegia)
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Spasticity and Rigidity
  • Arthritis (Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid arthritis)
  • Physiotherapy post joint replacement surgery
  • Homecare program

    • Everyone can benefit from physiotherapy whether you are living with a chronic illness, recovering from a work injury, or any sports injury.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    There is no fixed time duration. Every health condition or disorder is unique so does the treatment. Some of the patients need 2 to 3 weeks to restore their function and eliminate pain that occurred due to soft injury or post-op. At times, it is possible that patients take 6 to 8 weeks or even months or get recover from their condition (a rare condition). However, Physiotherapy helps in healing the patients for a long duration minimizing the risk of health complications in the future.

    A Physiotherapist can treat various issues like sports injuries, musculoskeletal, neurological issues, pediatric care and other problems as well depending upon their specialization. You can consult a Physical Therapist for neck and back pain, and problems in the bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments. Also, these specialists can treat fatigue, pain, swelling, stiffness and loss of muscle strength. Loss of mobility because of trauma to the brain (Stroke) or spine, or due to diseases such as Parkinson’s disease and multiple sclerosis.

    According to our experts, one should wear loose comfortable clothing. No matter what area of the body you are getting Physiotherapy sessions, wearing comforting clothes makes it easy for the patient and the therapist to perform exercises with or without using the equipment.
