Dr. Vijay Singh Chauhan

Chairman & Managing Director

Our Chairman Dr. V.S.Chauhan is an eminent Orthopaedic Surgeon & he has been instrumental in developing healthcare services in Noida since 1986. Prakash Hospital was conceived way back in 1994 and was inaugurated in February2001. He always believes that help & education must be the fundamental right of every citizen in a welfare state but India being such a largely populated country, Govt. alone cannot manage it so then comes the role of Private Enterprises in Health & Education. With this basic idea Prakash Hospital which is a 100 bedded Multispecialty hospital was conceived.

We are serving the society of Noida & adjoining areas satisfactorily for last 20yrs. We are going on adding more and more facilities to make it a compact & multidisciplinary hospital. Our motto is to provide quality Health care facilities to community at affordable cost with human touch.

About Prakash Hospital

Prakash Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a 100 bedded NABH NABL accredited multispecialty hospital along with a center of trauma and orthopedics. We are in the service of society since 2001. This hospital was founded by Dr. V S Chauhan who is an eminent  orthopedic       surgeon     and traumatologist and is giving services to the society since 1986. From the beginning our motto has been cure and care and we have tried our best to stick to this motto.
