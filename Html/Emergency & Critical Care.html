Department of Emergency and Critical Care

At Prakash Hospital, Noida the emergency and critical care department is managed by a highly qualified team of physicians, technicians, and nursing staff trained in a multidisciplinary approach to handling any critical situation in the shortest possible time. Over the years, the department excelled in adopting cutting-edge technology for providing advanced monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment to patients requiring immediate attention from the specialist. The department is fully equipped with state-of-the-art technologies and equipment to deal with all sorts of medical emergencies from life-threatening diseases, high-risk injuries (burns, road accidents), critical conditions (chest pain, stroke, myocardial infarction) to postoperative surgical complications.

The center aims at offering 24*7 emergency care services ranging from conventional medication to alternative therapies to deliver the finest treatment and interventions in emergency care.

Highlights of the department

  • A 23-bedded ICU equipped with advanced monitors and cutting-edge ventilators, Bipap and other equipment.
  • Round-the-clock personalized care under a highly skilled team of specialists, intensivists and nursing staff.
  • Maintain stringent high standards protocols 24*7 for patient safety.
  • Integrated dialysis facility for the patients in ICU.
  • Take all the necessary precautions to reduce ICU-acquired infections.
  • Ambulances are facilitated with the latest technology and manned by trained personnel.
  • Effective communication and coordinated emergency care throughout the hospitalization.

Services we offer:

  • Management of cardiac, neurology, orthopedics, and pediatrics emergencies.
  • Multi-disciplinary treatment facilities in case of emergencies such as road accidents, trauma, falls and assaults.
  • Prompt ambulance services, along with admission, evaluation and specialist consultation.
  • Arrangement of life-saving equipment like advanced stretchers, ventilators, pacemakers, oxygen cylinders, and other special requirements.
  • Expanded radiology services with X-Ray, MRI, Ultrasound, CT, ECG, EEG, EKG and Holter Monitoring.

We understand the importance of maintaining optimal emergency services so that anyone suffering from a debilitating condition should be treated right on time. At Prakash Hospital, we offer the best emergency and critical care services in Noida, with a commitment to improve and enhance the quality of patient care by addressing all the clinical problems and treatment facilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Often people get confused about when to go to the emergency room making it late to seek immediate attention. The experts of Prakash hospital suggest a list of warning signs that need emergency care including

  • Difficulty in breathing along with chest pain
  • Fainting, sudden dizziness, weakness
  • Uncontrolled bleeding after accident or injury
  • Numbness or stroke-like signs
  • Epilepsy or seizure attack
  • Persistent high-fever
  • Coughing or vomiting blood
  • Upper abdominal pain or pressure
  • Difficulty speaking
  • Symptoms observed for heart attack or cardiac arrest
  • Severe anxiety or sweating
  • Confusion or changes in mental status
  • High blood pressure or blood sugar levels
  • Animal bite

Usually, patients that require no tests may get discharged shortly after a thorough check-up. While in other cases, the specialist may order some tests to make a proper diagnosis and render treatment.

At first, you will be attended by triage nursing staff who will assess your health condition and run initial tests including blood tests, ECG, X-ray or check blood pressure and blood sugar levels. Meanwhile, patients will be treated by the specialized doctors and staff of emergency care. Emergency physicians are responsible for attending to all the patients according to their problems.
