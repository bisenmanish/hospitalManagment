About Prakash Hospital

Best Hospital in Noida

Prakash Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a 100 bedded NABH NABL accredited multispecialty hospital along with a center of Trauma and Orthopedics. We have been serving the society since 2001. This hospital was founded by Dr. V.S. Chauhan an eminent Orthopedic Surgeon and Traumatologist who has been serving the society since 1986. From the beginning our motto has been “cure and care” and we have tried our best to stick to realize it. Our mission is to provide affordable price to spread smile on everyone’s face. We have attended more than a million patients in our OPDs and have operated more than 50 thousand of patients during this span of 20 years with excellent results.

Our endeavour is to provide better and better service every day. The hospital boasts of two world-class major Operation Theaters, ICUs, Labour rooms, Private, Semi-private, and Economy wards.

We have NABL accredited laboratories with a very modern blood bank with component facilities. We have CT scan, Bone density (Dexa), Echo Cardio, USG, TMT. Along with that we have CT scan , Bone density (Dexa), Echo Cardio, USG, and TMT for the department of Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation. Prakash Hospitals’ modern & well-equipped Labour room and Nursery. We have 24 hours Pharmacy and Coffee shop along with a fleet of Ambulance in the service of patients. Our Hospital is listed on the panel of almost all public and private undertakings along with almost all TPAs

The hospital is centrally located in heart of Noida and easily approachable from all sides. It has plenty of parking space and is surrounded by greenery.
