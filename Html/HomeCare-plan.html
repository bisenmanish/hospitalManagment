About Prakash Hospital

Prakash Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a 100 bedded NABH NABL accredited multispecialty hospital along with a center of trauma and orthopedics. We are in the service of society since 2001. This hospital was founded by Dr. V S Chauhan who is an eminent  orthopedic       surgeon     and traumatologist and is giving services to the society since 1986. From the beginning our motto has been cure and care and we have tried our best to stick to this motto.
