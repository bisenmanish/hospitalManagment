Department of Urology

Urology is the specialty of medicine concerned with the study of male and female urinary tract system (kidneys, ureters, bladder and urethra) and male reproductive organs (penis, testes, scrotum, and prostate). Ageing, hereditary factors, infections/ injuries, hormonal imbalance or underlying illness can affect the functioning leading to a range of symptoms including pain, swelling, restricted or excessive urine flow, etc.

Our department of Urology is well-established and renowned in Noida, for offering excellent care with a human touch. The unit of urology is well-facilitated with a team of highly trained doctors having an outstanding hold over the latest advancements providing diagnostic and surgical services for all genitourinary disorders.

Why Choose Us?

The Department of Urology at Prakash Hospital, Noida is backed by a team of highly qualified urologists and uro-surgeons having thorough exposure to the management of illnesses of the male and female urinary tract and reproductive system. There are many urologic that are easier to be prevented when detected right on time. Owing to the support of other specialties, we led a comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for urinary infection, trauma or urinary obstruction, kidney stones, neurourology (nervous system control of genitourinary organs), Calculi (urinary tract stones), BPH (enlarged prostate), overactive bladder, male infertility and female urology.

We take pride in the fact that a large number of patients across Delhi, NCR and Noida rely upon our quality care and services. Over years, our team worked hard to make the unit a place of trust for many searching for a cure for their challenging urological conditions which make them hesitant the right way without resorting to surgery as the ultimate solution which evokes a sense of security in patients. If required, we are equipped with minimally invasive and laser techniques to provide patient-centric care at all costs.
