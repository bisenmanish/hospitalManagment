Best Paediatric Hospital in Noida

Every child is prone to catching an infectious disease or is vulnerable to getting any genetic disorder during their growing years. Pediatrics is a medical specialty that focuses on the health and well-being of children from birth through their adolescence. The main goal of the specialty is to aid children in offering a healthy and disease-free lifestyle leading them to become healthy adults. From everyday illnesses like cold & flu to serious childhood diseases and conditions like congenital heart disorder, and diabetes, children need precise prevention and management under the supervision of the best pediatrician.

Prakash Hospital, Noida has taken a big step in providing all-around comprehensive medical care to the little ones. Our team consists of top child specialists in Noida making it easier for the parents to take a sigh of relief while trusting the expertise of our doctors for their children’s health issues.

Why Choose Us?

The department of pediatrics is a top-notch unit in Noida offering a range of child care services for newborns to teenagers from medicinal, and surgical to immunization, and so on. Our experts put forward extraordinary dedication and expertise to take care of your child’s physical, social, emotional, and psychological development. The department is led by Dr. Vikas Kumar Agarwal, a senior pediatrician in Prakash Hospital, Noida has a wide experience of more than 15 years of special care for newborns and quality treatment of children.

The team of pediatricians is all experienced in offering expert counseling and consultations to the parents of newborns especially for managing challenges such as breastfeeding, burping, bathing, clothing, massage, care of umbilical stump, etc. Our neonatal unit is specially equipped with the facilities and trainers to solve the queries of patients ensuring that their kids are being attended to with the utmost compassion. Adding to that, we have skilled professionals treating a variety of pediatric diseases and illnesses related to skin, blood, lungs, and kidney. The department of pediatrics finely focuses on avoiding unnecessary medicines and preserving minimum medicines suiting to a child’s overall health.
