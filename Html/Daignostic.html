Department of Radiology & Lab Test

Radiology is a branch of medicine that uses imaging technology to diagnose and treat disease by performing radioscopy techniques such as X-Ray, ultrasound, MRI, and so on. At Prakash Hospital, Noida we perform some of the most advanced tests using hi-tech equipment helping in detecting life-threatening diseases. The radiology department is fully facilitated with the multislice CT scan, digital X-Ray, MRI, Bone Mineral Density Test (BMD), and advanced ultrasound offering exceptional quality images and accurate results.

We have the first Bone Densitometry machine DEXA from Wipro GE dedicated to measuring vitals such as calcium and bone minerals in the patients having the risk of OSTEOPOROSIS and other bone-related conditions.

In addition to the radioscopy, the department boasts world-class pathology services ensuring to deliver real-time reports of their patient’s conditions that further helps physicians recommend effective medical treatment. Our center is fully NABH & NABL accredited throughout every sector along with highly trained and experienced technicians, and skilled radiologists who are dedicated to providing 24*7 quality services to both IPD and OPD patients.

Why Choose Us?

Striving to upgrade with the progressing technology, the Department of Radiology and Laboratory Medicine is supported by high-class latest equipment designed by proven brands of the industry. Also, the team of pathologists and microbiologists are assigned from the renowned medical institutes of the country.

The entire staff of the laboratory and radiology center including nurses, attendants, and doctors follows the strict code of conduct carrying strong work ethics with a belief in delivering excellent diagnostic services. The technology platforms and SOPs (Standard operating procedures) of the lab have been revised and updated regularly by the top management.

Highlights of the department

  • Our radiologists and technicians specialize in imaging techniques like CT-scan, MRI, PET, nuclear medicine, X-ray, ECG, EKG, biopsy and ultrasound.
  • Our experts are known for interpreting the diagnosis and suggesting proper treatment under the required specialization.
  • We have a team to manage intervention radiology that assists in surgical procedures.
  • Our trained lab technologists follow standardized guidelines to deliver consistent and reliable lab reports.
  • Our lab offers home sample collection facilities, conducted by our trained and experienced phlebotomists.
